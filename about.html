About Us

Engeneering Sciences Institute for Training offer specialized education and training programs tailored specifically for the oil and gas industry. This include comprehensive courses, certifications, and workshops that provide in-depth knowledge and skills relevant to the industry. ESIT content is developed in collaboration with industry experts and professionals, ensuring that it is up-to-date, relevant, and aligned with the latest industry practices and technologies. This could provide a competitive edge over generic educational platforms, as it caters specifically to the unique needs and requirements of the Mining And oil and gas industry.

Engineering Sciences Institute for training is an international oil and gas training and consulting institute that aims to increase students and young professionals' technical understanding of the oil and gas industries from fundamentals to advanced levels through virtual & in-house courses, internships, workshops, etc. The training is designed to provide participants with a one-of-a-kind opportunity to impart the finest knowledge of technological advancements in the field along with the potential pathways for research which will lead to industrial innovation Our customers enable the opportunity to make connections with eminent professionals, academicians from prestigious universities, and consultants from top-notch agencies to provide cut-through expertise in order to assist organizations to achieve stronger efficiency in terms of reducing operating expenses and raising HSSE standards.
