About Us

Welcome to PEMAG (Petroleum, Energy, Mining & Agriculture) Company. We are a premier provider of comprehensive services in the petroleum, mining, energy, and agriculture sectors. Our offerings include training, inspections, consulting, auditing, and risk assessment in Health, Safety, and Environment (HSE). At PEMAG, we collaborate with leading companies and governmental ministries worldwide to deliver our expert services wherever they are needed. Our global reach and extensive industry expertise make us a trusted partner in advancing the safety, efficiency, and sustainability of your operations. Explore our services and discover how PEMAG can support your business with world-class solutions.

At our start-up company, we specialize in the oil, gas, and mining sectors while also focusing on energy and agriculture. Our team consists of experts from around the globe, including the USA, Algeria, Portugal, France, and Tunisia. We provide a range of services, including consultation, advisory, training, and auditing, tailored to meet the needs of our clients. Collaborating with international companies, we ensure that our solutions are innovative and effective. We work closely with ministries, universities, and laborers to promote best practices in our fields. Our mission is to foster sustainable development and implement cutting-edge technologies. Through our extensive network and expertise, we aim to deliver impactful results for every project. Together, we strive to make a significant contribution to our industries. Your success is our commitment.
